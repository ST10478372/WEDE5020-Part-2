About NetWave Solutions

Learn more about our mission, values, and the team behind South Africa's leading wireless internet provider.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, NetWave Solutions began with a simple mission: To bring reliable, high-speed internet to underserved areas of South Africa. This small operation in Mpumalanga gives a promise to growing into a leading Wireless Internet Service Provider (WISP) serving thousands of customers across the region.

We believe that everyone deserves access to quality internet connectivity, regardless of their location. Our team of dedicated professionals works tirelessly to expand our network and improve our services to meet the growing demands of our customers.

Today, NetWave Solutions is proud to be at the forefront of wireless technology innovation in South Africa, continually investing in infrastructure and training to deliver the best possible service to our community.
